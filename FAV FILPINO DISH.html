MY FAVORITE FILIPINO RECIPES

In this list, I will be discussing my top 3 favorite Filipino cuisine dishes.

Filipino cuisine is known for its vibrant and bold flavors, and these dishes are a true representation of the country's culture and heritage.

My Top 3 Favorite Filipino Dishes


  • Adobo

    Adobo is a popular Filipino dish made with meat (usually chicken or pork), vinegar, soy sauce, and garlic. The dish is typically cooked low and slow, resulting in a tender and flavorful meal that is often served with rice.

  • Kare-kare

    Kare-kare is a hearty Filipino stew that features tender oxtail, beef, or tripe in a rich and savory peanut sauce. The dish is typically served with bagoong, a fermented shrimp paste, and a side of steamed rice. Kare-kare is known for its unique combination of sweet, salty, and nutty flavors, making it a popular comfort food in the Philippines.

  • Sinigang

    Sinigang is a Filipino soup or stew characterized by its sour and savory taste. It is most often associated with tamarind.
